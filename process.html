Join the Digital Literacy Olympiad 2024!

Participants in the Digital Literacy Olympiad 2024 have the opportunity to earn prestigious awards and recognition. Advancing to the Semi-Final Round, successful participants will be awarded a Semi-Finalist Certificate. The top performers in the Final Round will be honored with digital medals and receive a 100 birr airtime package. These awards celebrate your achievements and commitment to excellence in digital literacy.
